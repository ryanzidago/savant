# 01 — Organisational Alignment

> Does this work align with the broader org goals, and is it the right thing to be working on right now?

## Why

Building the right thing matters more than building the thing right. Misaligned work is waste that compounds.

## Questions

- Is this the highest-leverage thing we could be working on right now?
- Does this support a current company or team objective, or is it speculative?
- Has the problem been validated with users, stakeholders, or data?
- Are we solving this at the right scope — not too narrow, not too ambitious?
- Is there an existing initiative this overlaps with or conflicts with?
- Have we communicated the intent and expected outcome to the right people?
- Will we be able to measure whether this was worth doing?
